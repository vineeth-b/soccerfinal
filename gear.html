In order to join and play for this club, you would need to buy the neccessary equipment. This will help keep your child safe from injuries. We will give out jersey, socks, water bottles, soccer balls, and goalie gloves.

Equipment:

Cleats(Soccer Boots)

Shin Pads

Sports Glasses(only if your child wears glasses)

If you don't know where to buy these equipment, you can visit Nike, Adidas and Sportchek for the best quality.
